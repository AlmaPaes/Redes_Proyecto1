Dogs of the month

Daimond

Diamond is like a bottle of champagne; bubbly and sweet, and always something to look forward to. She is an exuberant little lady, and is more than ready for a home of her own. We think she is around 6 years old, and she looks like a Pit Bull mix. Diamond is bouncy and exuberant and would do fantastic in a home with kids and potentially another dog.

Maca

Female. 1 year old. Big size. It has a lot of energy. Highly sociable with people. She is a bit dominant with dogs but can socialize without problems. She requires constant physical activity and it is highly recommended that you don't let her spend too much time alone.

Tessa

Tessa is a 5 year old Heeler mix. Loyal and attentive, as her heeler breed indicates. She prefers friends of the canine family. Tessa has waited awhile to meet her forever family. She is a wonderful companion and is ready for love.

Welcome to Get a DOGGO

Our mission is to save, socialize and secure loving homes for unwanted or abandoned dogs. Get a DOGGO accomplishes this goal by providing the highest standard of care for dogs in need with a particular focus on ensuring that all aspects of their overall health and well-being are addressed.
Our vision is to create a community where there is a home for every dog.
